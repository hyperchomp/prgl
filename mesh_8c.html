Creates a cube sphere.

Generates a sphere constructed from a cube by subdividing and expanding the six faces.

Parameters
resolutionThe resolution for the subdivision of each face. The number of quads per face will be this value squared, for example if resolution is 2 there will be 4 quads per face.
textureThe texture to assign to the mesh, or PRGL_NO_TEXTURE.
